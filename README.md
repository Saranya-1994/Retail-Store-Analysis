# Retail-Store-Analysis
Data-driven retail sales analysis uncovering customer behavior, pricing strategy, and revenue optimization insights

## 📊 Project Overview
This project analyzes 5,000+ retail transactions to uncover business insights related to customer behavior, pricing strategy, and revenue optimization.

## 🎯 Objective
- Improve customer retention
- Optimize pricing strategy
- Identify revenue-driving categories
- Analyze seasonal trends

## 📁 Dataset
- 5,000 retail transactions
- Features:
  - Customer demographics
  - Purchase amount
  - Product category
  - Discounts
  - Ratings
  - Payment methods

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- PowerPoint (Business Presentation)

## 📈 Key Insights
- 💰 Electronics contributes ~63% of total revenue
- 👥 Repeat customers spend 91% more than new customers
- 🎯 Optimal discount range: 11–15%
- 👩 Female segment shows $400K revenue opportunity
- 📅 Spring is the highest revenue season

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus on customer retention strategies
- Optimize discount strategy (avoid heavy discounting)
- Expand female-targeted product lines
- Improve category diversification


## 🚀 Outcome
- Identified ~$500K+ annual business opportunity
- Improved pricing & retention strategy
